About Us

Where Every Jump Meets a Delicious Bite

Welcome to Bounce and Bites – your one-stop destination for non-stop fun and mouthwatering food! Nestled inside our lively trampoline fun park, we’re more than just a snack bar — we’re part of the thrill, the energy, and the unforgettable memories.

At Bounce and Bites, we know that big jumps build big appetites. That’s why our kitchen is always fired up to serve the kind of food that keeps the fun going — quick, tasty, and full of flavor.

But we’re not just about the food — we’re about the experience. We believe every visit should be exciting from the moment you step in. So whether you're fueling up between flips, relaxing while the kids play, or just stopping by for a tasty meal, we’ve got the perfect mix of good vibes and great bites.
